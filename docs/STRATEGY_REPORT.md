# STRATEGY REPORT

## Market Positioning
Fiko Connect is the premium "Sales OS" for the African SME market, leveraging WhatsApp as the primary communication and payment channel.

## Revenue Model
1.  **SaaS Subscription:** Monthly plans (Terra, Mars, Krypton, Galaxy).
2.  **Transaction Fees:** 1% on Fiko Pay processed payments.
3.  **AI Credits:** Usage-based billing for high-volume users.

## Strategic Advantage
*   Native Mobile Money integration (Wave, Orange).
*   Hyper-localized AI models (Context aware of local business practices).
